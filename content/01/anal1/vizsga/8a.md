## $\frac{\sin(x)}{x}$ határértéke $0$-ban
$$\lim_{x\to0}\frac{\sin(x)}{x}=1\quad\quad\bigg(\frac{0}{0}\Rightarrow\text{L'Hopital}\bigg)$$
$$\lim_{x\to0}\frac{\sin(x)}{x}=\lim_{x\to0}\frac{\sin(x)'}{x'}=\frac{cos(x)}{1}=\frac{1}{1}=1$$
